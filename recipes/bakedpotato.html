Baked Potato

Baked Potato Description

In this blog page i will teach you how to create the most delicous baked potato!

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Sour Cream
  • Bacon Bits
  • Shredded Cheese 'any of your choosing'
  • Butter
  • Salt

Recipe Steps

  1. Heat up your baked potato till warm and soft
  2. Cut potato down the middle and lather butter on the insides
  3. Put a pinch on salt on the potato
  4. Spread in your cheese
  5. Dabble in your bacon bits on top
  6. Lastly put a spoonful of sour cream over the potato and enjoy!
