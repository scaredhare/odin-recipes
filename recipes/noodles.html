Cajun Pasta

Cajun Pasta Description

This blog will teach you how to create the most mouth watering cajun pasta you've ever seen!

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Heavy Whipping Cream
  • Cream Cheese
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onion & Garlic Powder
  • Cajun Seasoning
  • Minced Garlic
  • Shredded Cheese 'Any choice will work'

Recipe Steps

  1. Boil your noodles
  2. Put your minced garlin, cream cheese, and butter in a pan
  3. Cook until golden
  4. Add in heavy whipping cream and all seasonings
  5. Bring pan to low simmer
  6. Add in noodles and cheese
  7. Mix and then enjoy!
